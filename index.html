Event Details

Dates: 12 – 16 May 2025

Location: Cabourg, Normandy, France

Venue: Sweet Home Cabourg

Main Theme: Arithmetic geometry in a broad sense with emphasis on non-Archimedean degenerations, arithmetic dynamics, and Arakelov geometry.

Organizers: François Ballaÿ, Jérôme Poineau, and Robert Wilms (Université de Caen Normandie).

Registration: Registration is closed.

Travel Information

Arrival – Sunday 11 May 2025

To arrive in Cabourg by train from Paris, there are two options from Paris Saint-Lazare to Cabourg (with a stop in Trouville-Deauville):

  • Option 1: Paris Saint-Lazare 15h25 > Trouville-Deauville 18h11 and then Trouville-Deauville 18h54 > 19h24
  • Option 2: Paris Saint-Lazare 18h25 > Trouville-Deauville 20h48 and then Trouville-Deauville 20h53 > 21h23 (risky: only five minutes to change in Deauville)

Departure – Friday 16 May 2025

From Cabourg to Paris, one can take a bus from Cabourg to Deauville (approximately 45 minutes) and then a train from Deauville to Paris (there are no direct trains from Cabourg to Deauville).

The bus service is provided by "Nomad Car Ligne 111". The bus ticket costs €2.90 and can be purchased directly from the driver (cards and cash accepted). For the bus schedule, please refer to the Nomad Car Ligne 111 Schedule.

Departure Possibilities:

  • Bus Cabourg 12h02 > Deauville 12h43 and Train Deauville-Trouville 13h42 > Paris Saint-Lazare 16h31 (one change in Lisieux)
  • Bus Cabourg 15h07 > Deauville 15h48 and Train Deauville-Trouville 16h20 > Paris Saint-Lazare 18h29
  • Bus Cabourg 16h03 > Deauville 16h49 and Train Deauville-Trouville 17h22 > Paris Saint-Lazare 19h30
  • Bus Cabourg 17h45 > Deauville 18h26 and Train Deauville-Trouville 19h17 > Paris Saint-Lazare 21h36 (one change in Lisieux)

If you are traveling at a different time and are interested in carpooling, please let us know.
